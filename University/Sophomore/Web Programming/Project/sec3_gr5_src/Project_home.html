Women collection

Inspired by the idea of "telling a beautiful story", self-taught designer Simon Porte Jacquemus launched his own womenswear line at just 19. His label quickly gained the support of Rei Kawakubo and it has gone on to become one of the biggest hits in modern French fashion. Expect innovative silhouettes, unexpected motifs and playful details from this cult Gallic brand.

Men collection

‘I don’t just do clothes — I write a story and then come the clothes.’ This winning formula has propelled Simon Porte Jacquemus from self-taught designer to one of Paris’s most talked-about talents. His sunshine-filled Provençal roots reign supreme across dresses, blazers and bucket hats, crafting recognizable Alzou cardigans and, of course, the now-iconic Le Chiquito bag.

Kid collection

With more than 90 years of design history, Fendi children have reinvented themselves as ambassadors for the 'fearless', the self-confessed 'freaks' and the forever 'free' as part of the label's 'F is for...' campaign.
